FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions here

What is an ERP system and why it is important for businesses in Dubai?

An ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system integrates all your business processes—finance, HR, sales, and supply chain—into one platform. In Dubai, it is crucial for ensuring compliance with local regulations like ZATCA and VAT, while streamlining operations for faster growth.

Do employees need special training to use an ERP system?

While some initial orientation is helpful, modern ERP systems like ours are built with user-friendly interfaces that mimic common apps. Most teams can get up to speed very quickly with minimal training.

Which industries benefit the most from ERP system?

Industries such as retail, manufacturing, logistics, and professional services see the biggest impact. Any business that handles high volumes of transactions or complex inventory will benefit from automation and centralized data.

Is the ERP fully compliant with UAE VAT and the upcoming 2026 E-Invoicing mandate?

Absolutely. Our system is designed to meet all current UAE FTA requirements for VAT and is being proactively updated to support Phase 2 of the ZATCA e-invoicing mandate and future UAE e-invoicing laws in 2026.

How much is the real total cost?

We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. The total cost typically includes your subscription fee and optional setup support. Contact us for a detailed demo and tailored quote for your specific needs.
